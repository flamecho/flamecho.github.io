◈ 故事梗概

世界滅亡が予言された年、東京
Tokyo, during the year in which the world was prophesied to end.

空想と心中する勇気もなく
As we clung to reality,
現実にしがみついている僕たちは
too spineless to throw our lives away together,
マミヤと　出会う
we met Mamiya.

友人の葬式　弔う人々
At a friend’s funeral, the entire crowd was weeping and mourning,
しかし　人がひとり死んだところで世界は変わらず
while we thought to ourselves that the death of a single person had no meaningful impact on the world at large.
目が覚めれば　同じ日々の反芻
We slept, woke up, and went through the same daily grind as ever.
――そう　思っていた
At least, that’s how it was supposed to be.

マミヤが　現れるまでは
Then Mamiya appeared…

目の前に現れた“マミヤ”と名乗る謎の子ども
Before we even knew it, the mysterious child named Mamiya had wormed their way into our daily lives.
次第にそれは日常を侵食し　僕たちの拠り所となっていく
They slowly, but surely, became something of an emotional support. They’d brought us down a new path.

その先に　死が待っていようとも
A path that would surely lead us to our deaths…
